A Day of Adventure: School Picnic at Explorer Ranch

Students from Class 5 to 8 embark on a thrilling adventure to Explorer Ranch in Entebbe. With their teachers and Principal, they enjoy a delightful picnic filled with sunshine and laughter.

Capturing every moment with photographs, they create memories of swinging, playing board games, and exploring the beautiful surroundings.

As they bond over games and hikes, the students relish the joy of childhood, leaving behind the worries of school for a day of pure fun.

The school picnic becomes a cherished memory, reminding them of the simple joys shared with friends.
